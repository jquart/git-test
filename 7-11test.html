Good Humor 7-11 Store Takeover

Good Humor partnered with 7-Eleven in NY, Boston, DC and Miami to drive impulse snackers to choose Good Humor Ice Cream.

How it works: Drive volume and provide added value to 7-Eleven shoppers with a “neighborhood joy” sweepstakes that rewards shoppers who treat themselves to Good Humor.

  • With every purchase of Good Humor products at 7-Eleven, shoppers have a chance to win when they text a photo of their receipt.
  • Promote the sweeps on handouts at the sampling events, in-store POS and OOH media
  • Everyone who participates will be entered for a chance to win tickets to local pastimes in each market (MLB tickets, Six Flags, Legoland tickets, etc.)
